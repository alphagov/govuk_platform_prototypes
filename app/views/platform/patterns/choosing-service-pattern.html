Different services pattern meet different user and government needs.

Register a thing

Single record of an activity or thing which an individual or organisation is responsible for.

Creates a single reference for a activiy or thing linked to a individual or organisation accountable for it.

Licensing

Authorisations by government for an individual or organisation to do something (or not do something).

A licence is a barrier or a checkpoint to making sure someone does the right thing.

Funding

Suporting research with potential benefits that are not commercial or not long term

Encourage research with long term benefits for society, but is too risky for private sector to back.

Ownership exchange

Transfer responsibility for a registered thing between an individual or organisation and another.

Creates a history of individuals and organisations accountable for a controlled thing and it's use.

Delegate responsibility

Give permission to another person to carry out a activity on your behalf.

Allows professionals or others to make decisions for people, when they are unable or willing to themselves.

Enforcement

Targeted checks that someone is doing the right thing, in the agreed way.

Limit the risk and damage when someone knowingly does the wrong right thing.
